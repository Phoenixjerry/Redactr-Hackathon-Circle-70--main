About The App

Redact /rɪˈdakt/ : censor or obscure (part of a text) for legal or security purposes.

The app is to help people safely share content on social media by scrambling portions of the text they don’t want the world to read/see. A user will write/paste the some content into a form field and then specify the words they need to be scrambled (using another field). Note the user is allowed to specify more than one word (separated by space).
